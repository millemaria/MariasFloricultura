Sobre produto:

Lilies are bulbous plants that belong to the Liliaceae family and are prized for their beautiful, large, showy flowers. There are many different varieties of lilies, including Asiatic lilies, oriental lilies, trumpet lilies, lilies of the valley, and more. Each variety has its own distinct characteristics such as size, shape, color and fragrance.

Lily flowers usually grow on tall, upright stems with long, narrow leaves that extend the length of the stem. The flowers have six trumpet-shaped petals, with a wide variety of colors including white, pink, yellow, orange, red and more. Some varieties of lilies are fragrant and give off a sweet, pleasant scent.
